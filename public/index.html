About

The Loving Hearts Organization is a registered CBO founded by David Mutie in 2022. The main idea that brought about its establishment was to assist the vulnerable in the society. Targeting the less fortunate across all sectors in the society. Initially, the organization had around 20 members but the number has rose significantly over the years. We have reached out to as many people in the society spreading love to the less fortunate. In partnership with other CBO we aim to reach higher levels.

Projects

Carnival of Love Season 1

Our first activity since the organization began. We reached out to around 20 families and donated foodstuffs and sanitary. We partnered with other CBOs to reach out to these families.

Carnival of Love Season 2

Reached out to 50 vulnerable families in Soweto and donated foodstuff. This was in collaboration with other CBOs. Event was held at St. Joachim and Ann Parish in the slums of Soweto.
